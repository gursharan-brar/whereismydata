04

HOW IT WORKS

01

Paste the policy

Open the company's privacy policy in another tab. Copy the text. Paste it into the scanner. No URL fetching, no scraping — you're in control of what gets read.

02

Claude reads it

Your browser sends the policy plus a rubric grounded in PIPEDA's ten fair information principles to Claude Haiku. Claude scores eleven criteria as yes, partial, or no.

03

You get a report

Weighted score becomes a letter grade. Each criterion gets a one-line note plus a short evidence quote. Disclaimer attached. The whole loop runs in your browser tab.
